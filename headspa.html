Why Choose Myeongdong Headspa at LEEKAJA?

LEEKAJA Myeongdong is the premier destination for head spa in Myeongdong, Seoul. Located just 3 minutes from Myeongdong Station Exit 8, our Myeongdong Headspa offers 5 professionally designed scalp treatment programs using premium Korean ingredients. Whether you're a tourist visiting Seoul or a local resident, our 明洞ヘッドスパ provides a deeply relaxing and effective scalp care experience with English and Japanese speaking staff.

🔬 AI Scalp Diagnosis

Before and after each session, our advanced scalp diagnosis device analyzes your scalp condition — oiliness, dryness, dandruff, and hair loss risk — giving you personalized care.

🌊 Waterfall Therapy

Experience LEEKAJA's signature Aroma Waterfall and Sparkling Waterfall therapy — a unique sensory treatment that deeply cleanses pores while providing total relaxation.

🌿 Premium Korean Ingredients

We use PGA (Poly Glutamic Acid) serums, 17-amino acid scalp packs, laurel aroma mist, and carbonated serums — all professional-grade products for maximum scalp health.

🗣️ Multilingual Staff

Our team fluently speaks English and Japanese. Tourists from all over the world trust LEEKAJA Myeongdong for a comfortable, communication-friendly head spa experience in Seoul.

📍 Best Location in Myeongdong

3F, 22 Myeongdong 8ga-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul. Just 3 minutes walk from Myeongdong Station Exit 8. Easy to find, perfect for tourists exploring Myeongdong.

⚡ Advanced Devices (Reborn Spa)

Our premium Reborn Head Spa uses Radio Frequency (고주파) for scalp tightening and Plasma Beauty Device (플라즈마) for collagen production and anti-inflammation.

Myeongdong Headspa FAQ

자주 묻는 질문 | よくある質問

How much does Myeongdong Headspa cost?
LEEKAJA Myeongdong Headspa offers 5 programs: Relaxing Spa ₩88,000, Power Head Spa ₩120,000, Energy Head Spa ₩154,000, Youth Head Spa ₩198,000, Reborn Head Spa ₩228,000.
Where is the Myeongdong Headspa? How do I get there?
3F, 22 Myeongdong 8ga-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul. Exit Myeongdong Station (Line 4) from Exit 8 and walk straight for 3 minutes. Look for the LEEKAJA sign on the 3rd floor.
Do you need a reservation for Myeongdong Headspa?
Walk-ins are welcome but reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends. Contact us via WhatsApp (+82-10-5894-7690) or call 02-777-8322. Open daily 10:00–20:00.
How long does the head spa take?
Relaxing Spa ~40 min, Power Head Spa ~60 min, Energy / Youth / Reborn Head Spa ~90 min each.
Do you speak English or Japanese?
Yes! Our staff speak English and Japanese fluently. LEEKAJA Myeongdong is a popular choice for tourists and foreign residents in Seoul.
明洞ヘッドスパの料金はいくらですか？
LEEKAJAの明洞ヘッドスパは₩88,000（リラクシングスパ）から₩228,000（リボーンヘッドスパ）まで5種類のプログラムをご用意しています。日本語対応スタッフが丁寧にご案内します。
명동 헤드스파 예약은 어떻게 하나요?
WhatsApp (+82-10-5894-7690), 전화 (02-777-8322), 또는 LINE (kbeautyseoul)으로 예약하실 수 있습니다. 워크인도 가능하지만 주말에는 예약을 권장합니다.
